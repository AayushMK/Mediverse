About MVTL

Welcome to Mediverse Tradelink Pvt Ltd (MVTL) – a dynamic force in Nepal's healthcare sector, specializing in the import and distribution of high-quality and innovative surgical products. With a vision to expand into nutritional supplements and pharmaceuticals, we are committed to evolving alongside Nepal's growing medical needs.

Our Journey

Founded in 2025 by four enthusiast, MVTL plan to grow from a small distributor to one of the Nepal's most trusted medical supply partners, serving over 250 healthcare institutions nationwide.

Market Position

We are committed to securing 25% market share in Nepal's surgical product sector, backed by a robust distribution network spanning 50+ districts. By ensuring reliable access to high-quality medical supplies, we empower healthcare providers to deliver exceptional patient care nationwide. Recognizing the shift toward preventive healthcare, MVTL will launch a dedicated nutraceutical division in 2026. Our mission? To offer scientifically backed wellness solutions that help Nepali communities thrive—before illness strikes. Beyond surgical and nutraceutical excellence, MVTL is laying the groundwork to enter the pharmaceutical sector. We're not just observing Nepal's unmet medical needs—we're engineering solutions to turn them into reality.
